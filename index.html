Who to vote for in the primary, ranked by who is likely to beat Trump in swing states.
How is the score computed?

For each candidate look at how they're doing in the best rated general election poll against Trump from FiveThirtyEight for each swing state.

Use their favored poll percentage multiplied by the number of delegates for each swing state. Sum them and choose a winner.

Known problems

Date of the poll is not considered, just the ranking. In practice, most of the polls aren't moving significantly and aren't super old. If I get enough backlash I'll write some date parsing code (yuck).
